PRIVACY POLICY

Black Store - Secure Dark Web Marketplace

1. Information Collection

Black Store operates under a zero-logging policy. We do not collect, store, or track any personal information including:

  • IP addresses or connection logs
  • Personal identification data
  • Payment information (Bitcoin transactions are anonymous)
  • Browsing history or session data
  • Email addresses or contact information

2. Anonymous Operations

Since 2020, Black Store has maintained complete anonymity for all users through:

  • Tor Network Integration: All communications encrypted via Tor
  • Bitcoin-Only Payments: No traceable payment methods accepted
  • No Registration Required: Access without creating accounts
  • Temporary Session IDs: Generated locally, not stored on servers

3. Data Protection

Our infrastructure ensures maximum privacy protection:

  • Servers located in privacy-friendly jurisdictions
  • End-to-end encryption for all communications
  • Regular server wipes and data destruction
  • No cooperation with law enforcement agencies
  • Distributed server architecture for enhanced security

4. Third-Party Services

Black Store does not integrate with any third-party tracking services, analytics platforms, or external data collectors. We maintain complete independence from:

  • Google Analytics or similar tracking services
  • Social media platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Government surveillance programs

5. Cookie Policy

We use minimal, essential cookies only for:

  • Maintaining shopping cart functionality (temporary)
  • Session management (deleted after browser closure)
  • Security measures against automated attacks

No tracking cookies or persistent identifiers are used.

6. Communication Security

All communications with Black Store are secured through:

  • PGP Encryption: Available for sensitive communications
  • Tor Messaging: Built-in encrypted chat system
  • Temporary Channels: Messages auto-deleted after 24 hours

7. Legal Compliance

Black Store operates in jurisdictions with strong privacy laws. We:

  • Do not maintain logs that could be subpoenaed
  • Cannot provide user data as we don't collect it
  • Operate under legal frameworks that protect user privacy
  • Maintain plausible deniability for all transactions

8. Policy Updates

This privacy policy has been in effect since January 2020 and is updated only to enhance user privacy protection. Any changes will be announced on our main page.

9. Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us through:

  • Encrypted Tor chat on our main page
  • PGP-encrypted messages (keys available on our PGP page)
  • Temporary secure channels (no permanent contact methods)
