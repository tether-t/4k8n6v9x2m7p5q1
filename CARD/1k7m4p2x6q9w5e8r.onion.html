Payment & Orders

How do I pay for products?
We accept Bitcoin only for maximum anonymity. Send the exact amount to the provided address. Payment is confirmed after 3 blockchain confirmations (usually 30-60 minutes).
Why is my payment not confirmed?
Payments require minimum 3 confirmations. Check your transaction on blockchain explorer. If you sent insufficient fees, it may take longer. Contact support if payment is pending over 2 hours.
Can I get a refund?
No refunds due to the digital nature of products. All sales are final. We guarantee 99.8% success rate. If product doesn't work, contact support for replacement.

Security & Privacy

Is Black Store safe to use?
Yes. We've operated safely since 2020 with zero security breaches. We use military-grade encryption, maintain no logs, and operate through Tor network for complete anonymity.
Do you keep logs of my activity?
Zero logging policy. We don't store IP addresses, browsing history, or personal data. All session data is automatically deleted after 24 hours.
How do I access the site securely?
Always use Tor Browser. Never access through regular browsers or VPNs. Verify our onion address. Use our PGP keys for sensitive communications.

Technical Support

Site won't load or is slow?
Try new Tor circuit (Ctrl+Shift+L). Clear browser cache. Use Tor bridges if blocked. Check if you're using latest Tor Browser version.
How to contact support?
Use live chat on main page (fastest), create support ticket, or send PGP-encrypted email. Average response time: 1-2 hours.
Can I use mobile devices?
Yes, but use Tor Browser for Android or Onion Browser for iOS. Never use regular mobile browsers for security reasons.
